Behind Team Adhirudh

We aim to reduce the negatives of the dyeing industry like water wastage, pollution due to effluent discharge, energy wastage etc for which traditional dyeing processes are responsible. We aim to give textile mills an economically viable, ecologically sustainable alternate in the form of foam dyeing.
